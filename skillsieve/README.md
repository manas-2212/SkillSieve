# 🧠 SkillSieve – AI-Powered Internship & Career Skill Match Platform

## 🚀 Overview
**SkillSieve** is an AI-powered platform that helps students and fresh graduates discover suitable internships and understand the skills they need to grow in their desired career paths.

Unlike traditional job portals, SkillSieve doesn’t just list internships — it **analyzes resumes, identifies missing skills, and provides personalized learning paths** using AI recommendations.

---

## 🧩 Problem Statement
Many students and early professionals struggle to:
- Identify internships that truly match their skills and goals.
- Understand what skills are missing for their dream roles.
- Find tailored learning resources for upskilling.

**SkillSieve bridges this gap** by intelligently matching users with internships and learning resources based on their current skill set, career aspirations, and AI-driven recommendations.
